> aica_sim

AICASim: A simulation of an activation/inhibition cellular automaton written in JavaScript. It models a network of connected cells under the influence of an activator chemical and an inhibitor chemical. Changing the two chemicals' area of influence and diffusion rate can create emergent behaviors such as the formation of patterns.

Demo / Github



> birds_eye

Bird's Eye: A course project done with a colleague of mine, Tim Goering, written in Java. Bird's Eye is an interactive visualization tool that allows users to manipulate streaming Twitter data sets by filtering data by keyword or time and then representing that data on an animated map. It also provides statistical summary information in the form of a line graph and bar graph that display the frequencies of related keywords.
